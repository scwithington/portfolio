Development Back on Track.

May 23, 2022

Now that the spring quarter is coming to an end, I will be able to focus more on game development. I have been considering the idea of using flat-shading to create a 2D look for the rogue-like. One option could be as simple as doing toon-shaders. I will continue to research this topic because I imagine it would work well with the theme of the game. Until I have the means to compile the game, I will continue soft development on paper.

Game Dev Progress Update:

I have begun the conception of new enemies although I have not begun the modeling/animation process for any of them. Learning more about flat 3D animation and UE4 shaders. Conception of enemy attack and scaling systems.

Check in soon for more updates!

Blog Post 1.0

April 20, 2022

At present, any game development projects have been put on hold due to lack of time. However, I am certainly enjoying my general studies at SCAD Savannah. It has given me opportunities to learn more about the creation of art with many different mediums such as ink & brush or artist's graphite. I also had a chance to update this site during a class project focusing on our digital personas.

Game Dev Progress Update:

Development of horror survival has halted due to issues with the engine version and outdated assets. My current project consists of a rogue-like game with simple souls-like gameplay. Animations for spear and sword movesets have already been implemented and I have made much progress in concept and storyboard but so far, only 2 enemies have been modelled and animated. Currently using the Polygon - Dungeon Pack from UE4 store as a world placeholder until further development.

More to come in the near future!
